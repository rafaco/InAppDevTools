for developers

Rich overlay layer with powerful inspection tools

  • Logs and events: Filter your standard Logcat output mixed with an auto generate a log of events covering from basic reproduction steps to advanced lifecycle entries.
  • Crash handler: Get full crash details immediately on the same screen it happen! Including screenshot, logs and navigation throw causing source lines.
  • Info panels: Exhaustive information about the device, your app, the current compilation and running things (processes, threads, tasks, services, providers...).
  • Source browser: AndroidManifest, resources, assets, Java/Kotlin sources and build time generated sources.
  • View inspector: Get info about current activity and fragments, with navigation to their sources and xml. Inspect elements by touch or throw the hierarchy and edit their values and see your changes directly.
  • Storage editor: Browse your internal databases, SharedPrefs and files. Hot edition allowed for DB and SharedPrefs.
  • Network: Inspect your request and responses when communicating with your backend or with any service throw Retrofit.
